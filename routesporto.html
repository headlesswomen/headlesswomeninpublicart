Routes in Porto


Headless Women Route

What does this mean to lose one's head? And one's mind? This route invites you to learn about the influence the Fine Arts School had on Porto's Public Art and on the education of women artists. It is also an invitation to get to know the historical Bonfim neighborhood under a different light.

Monuments and Women Route

From Justice to Pain: how women take part on Porto's Public Art? This route is an invitation to see, on the one hand, how frequently sculptors use the body of women to represent archetypes and, on the other hand, to draw attention to the low participation of women artists on the city's Public Art.

Public and Art Route

Why is Public Art important? This route calls for the debate on the concept and the roles of Public Art: For whom? By whom? About whom? Where? Discussing what values it can hold in the Public Sphere.
