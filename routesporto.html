Routes in Porto


Headless Women Route

What does it mean to lose one's head? And one's mind? The Headless Women Route invites you to learn about the influence the School of Fine Arts of the University of Porto, and its ancestors, had on The Public Art of the city of Porto and to learn about the education of women artists in Europe. This Route is also an invitation to get to know the historical neighborhood of Bonfim under a less common light.

Meeting point: Entrance hall of the School of Fine Arts of the University of Porto, Avenida Rodrigues de Freitas, 265 4049-021 - Porto
Duration: 60 min.
Free but subject to registration: headlesswomeninpublicart@gmail.com

Monuments and Women Route

From Justice to Pain: how do women take part on Porto's Public Art? The Monuments and Women Route invites you to think how frequently sculptors use the shapes and fragments of women's bodies to represent a variety of archetypes. This tour also reflects on the small number of hommages payed to women through monuments and sculptures and on the low participation of women artists in the city's Public Art.

Meeting point: Jardim da Cordoaria, in front of the Palace of Justice
Duration: 90 min.
Free but subject to registration: headlesswomeninpublicart@gmail.com

Public and Art Route

Why is Public Art important? This route calls for the debate on the concept and the roles of Public Art: For whom? By whom? About whom? Where? The tour offers an opportunity to discuss the values it can hold in the public sphere of a city.
